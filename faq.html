frequently asked questions

Here you can find the answers to the most frequently asked questions for DreamHack 2018. If you cant find your answer please contact the DreamHack Support via email at support@dreamhack.se. Allow up to 72 hours response time.

General

When does DreamHack open and close?

Doors open: 08:00 CEST, Wednesday 29th August
Opening ceremony: 16:00 CEST, Wednesday 29th August
The network goes down: 08:00 CEST, Sunday 2nd September
The electricity goes down: 08:40, CEST, Sunday 2nd September
All participants must be out of the Litexpo Center: 10:00 CEST, Sunday 2nd September
Between 08:00 CEST 29th August and 10:00 2nd September, DreamHack is open 24/7.

Tickets

Is there an age limit?

DreamHack 2018 is open for all ages but can contain content such as tournaments, exhibitors and partners showing games, and other material not suitable for all ages.

My ticket has not arrived, what can I do?

If the DreamHack 2018 ticket does not arrive, contact special booth in Lobby market TICKET SUPPORT. Everyone who has bought a valid ticket to DreamHack 2018 will get in once their ID has been confirmed. Make sure to be able to log in to our account at location.

My ticket is torn/damaged, what can I do?

Contact special booth in Lobby market TICKET SUPPORT. Everyone who has bought a valid ticket to DreamHack 2018 will get in once their ID has been confirmed, even if the ticket is torn or otherwise damaged. Make sure to be able to log in to our account at location.

I don't have a printer, can I show my ticket on phone/tablet?

Yes, that's fine.

Do you offer refunds on tickets?

No. DreamHack does not offer refunds on bought tickets and we do not offer the possibility to change the ticket for another event.

Can I buy tickets on site?

Yes! You can buy Event Passes and Day Passes at the Main Entrance at DreamHack 2018. We accept VISA, Mastercard and cash (SEK). If you want a Bring Your Own Computer Ticket (BYOC) you will have to pre order your ticket Online.

Are there any discounts for students?

Yes! You can buy DreamHack 2018 tickets with 25% discount if you provide International Student Identity Card (ISIC) ID code during online purchase or the physical card itself at the ticket booth.

Table & Seating

How much space is included for my BYOC seat?

All participants will get a table that is 83×60 cm. The height of the table is 82 cm.

Can I bring a shelf an put my computer screens on them?

Yes. Just make sure it fits on your available space and that is built sturdily so it will not be dangerous for you or any other participants. Note: All participants get 83×60 cm of table space.

Can I bring a refrigerator/coffee maker/water boiler?

No! The electricity in the LAN is built to handle your computer, screens and console; not kitchen appliances.

How long does Ethernet/TP/Cat5/Cat5E/Cat6 cable need to be?

If you bring 10 meters it will be enough to reach the switch on the table.

Can I bring my own chair?

Yes! DreamHack provides a simple chair to all participants. Feel free to bring your office chair or a pillow to sit more comfortably.

Can I bring my gaming console?

Yes! As long as you keep your stuff in your table space that is 83×60 cm.

Cosplay, Dressing Rooms & Logistics

Will there be a secure room to keep cosplay costumes and accessories before/after events?

Yes! There will be 2 halls, as well as 10 locker rooms with cameras and ID Card scanners to keep your cosplay attire.

Do I have to bring my cosplay costume in the evening/morning before main event starts?

No, you can bring your cosplay costume at any time you like, but keep in mind later in the day the venue will get crowded,so to avoid damage to the costume we advise you to bring it as early in the morning as possible.

Are dedicated dressing rooms for cosplayers?

Yes, there will be 2 dressing rooms for cosplayers to get ready before cosplay contests. The dressing rooms are limited to cosplay event participants only, cosplayers who will not be participating in the event will have to dress up in storage halls or locker rooms.

How long can I keep my cosplay attire at the venue?

We require you to take your cosplay attire as soon as the event ends, but based on the amount of cosplayers, we may extend retrieval time for up to 24 hours.

Is there a special entrance for cosplayers?

Yes, there is a special entrance for cosplayers straight into safekeeping areas, avoiding general growd in the building.

Other

Where do you sleep at DreamHack?

Dreamhack offers 4 hotels for sleeping during DreamHack 2018 in Vilnius. Hotels Novotel, Congress, Europa Hotel and Artis are all located near city center, and access to the hotels is granted based on ticket group.

Can I shower at DreamHack?

There are toilets and showers in the Hall D. We have a team that works 24/7 to keep the facilities clean and tidy.

Is there WiFi available?

Yes! But we strongly recommend connecting your main computer to the Internet via cable for the best and fastest experience.

Can I smoke electronic cigarettes inside of DreamHack?

No! All smoking, regular or electronic is only allowed in special smoking areas outside of the venue.
